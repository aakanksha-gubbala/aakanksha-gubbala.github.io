Arnold, D.P. (co-first), Gubbala, A. (co-first), Takatori, S.C. Active surface flows accelerate the coarsening of lipid membrane domains
arXiv Journal
This paper studies the coarsening of lipid membrane domains driven by active surface flows from motor-driven actin filaments. We find that these active flows accelerate domain growth, doubling the typical growth exponent compared to passive systems, and develop analytical models to explain the phenomenon.
